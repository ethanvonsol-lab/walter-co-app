**Goal:** Keep first clients happy, engaged, and unlikely to churn.  
**Timeline:** Day 1 → Day 30

## Day 1 — Onboarding Call

### During the Call (60 minutes)

**Structure:**
1. **Introduction** (5 min)
   - Welcome, energy, excitement
   - Quick overview of what's possible

2. **Discovery** (15 min)
   - What's your business? (service, product, audience)
   - How do you currently handle DMs? (manual, ignore, hire someone)
   - What's your biggest DM challenge? (time, missing leads, generic responses)
   - What does a "good" DM reply look like from you?

3. **Voice Profile Building** (25 min)
   - Go through the 10-question onboarding on your app together
   - Help them articulate their tone
   - Share real DM examples to illustrate
   - Refine their voice profile in real-time if needed

4. **Dashboard Setup** (10 min)
   - Show them the dashboard (inbox, leads, analytics, settings)
   - Show the conversation pause feature
   - Show how to review AI replies

5. **Set Expectations** (5 min)
   - "Week 1: Actively monitor. Review each reply."
   - "It's beta AI. Some replies might be off. Let us know."
   - "If you hate something, we'll adjust."
   - "You can pause AI per conversation anytime."

**Tone:** Friendly, consultative, not salesy. You're partners, not vendor/customer.

## Red Flags → Intervention

**If they:**
- Stop replying to check-ins
- Pause AI frequently (suggests dissatisfaction)
- Don't receive many DMs (might think it's not worth it)
- Report bad replies and we don't fix them quickly

**Intervention:**
1. **Day 7 No Response:** Send "Hey, want to jump on a quick call?"
2. **Day 14 No Response:** "Your month 1 is coming to an end. Let's chat about next steps."
3. **If they mention concerns:** Hop on a call within 24 hours to fix it

---

## Success Metrics

**By Day 30, Healthy Clients Should Have:**
- ✅ Received 50+ DMs (or at least 5-10)
- ✅ Captured 2-3 quality leads
- ✅ Given you positive feedback on at least 50% of AI replies
- ✅ Used the pause feature <10% (means they trust the AI)
- ✅ Engaged with at least 3 check-ins

**If they're hitting these:** You're likely to keep them. Focus on showing ROI (leads → sales conversion).

---

## Upsell Triggers (Month 2+)

Once they're past the 30-day mark and happy:

- **More accounts:** "Want to add a second Instagram account? $250 setup."
- **Agencies:** "Know someone who'd benefit? Refer them and get 30% commission."
- **Custom training:** "Your AI is solid. Want me to help you fine-tune it further?"
- **Advanced features:** [IF YOU BUILD THEM] "New feature: DM automation templates. Want in?"

---

## Notes

- **Be human.** Don't be a bot about this. Personalize emails.
- **Be responsive.** If they email, reply within 24 hours.
- **Celebrate wins.** When they get a lead, act excited.
- **Own mistakes.** If the AI messes up, apologize and fix it immediately.
- **Ask for feedback.** Every interaction, ask: "What could we do better?"
- **Be generous.** If they're on the fence, offer an extra week free or a custom call — retention costs less than acquisition.
